ABOUT ISLAND

Rainbow magic park is amusement park and water park located in north Delhi,India.The park is owned and operated by Entertainment City Limited, a joint venture of International Amusement Limited and Unitech Holdings Ltd.Incorporated on 7th January 2018.*147.48 acres of land allotted by DDA on a 90 years lease • 85% of the land earmarked for amusement park and ancillary activities; rest 15% for commercial activities. • Total project FAR of 3.63 million sqft (2.18 million sqft in Park and 1.44 million sqft in commercial)

Facilities

Food Court

Enjoy a variety of dining options at the Rainbow Magic Park. It has several restaurants and food counters for you to grab a bite while you are in the park.It also offers range of cuisines and delicious dining options at reasonable rates in the park.

Spa Area

A spa is a location where mineral-rich spring water (and sometimes seawater) is used to give medicinal baths.Health Office that is responsible for planning public health and clinical services according to the health needs of local communities.

Dormitory

Rainbow Magic Park provides safe, convenient and affordable overnight lodging for students visiting park as part of a school excursion program.Experience ultimate relaxation in the lap of nature.

Lockers

Lockers and costumes are available on rent.Rainbow Magic Park management will not be responsible for any loss of articles.The locker price is Rs 300 (including Rs. 200 refundable)
